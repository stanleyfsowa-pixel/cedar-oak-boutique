Our Story in New Lenox, Illinois

Our boutique is a unique and ever changing mix of local artisans, vintage finds, unique furniture, and fresh new gifts you will not find anywhere else. From hand poured candles and one of a kind jewelry to standout seasonal decor, every corner is filled with personality. We love bringing people together with art classes, maker meet-ups, and lively pop-up events that turn our shop into a community pillar.
